About Us

Our History

Willow Academy is a private university in Nigeria (Africa). It was established in 2010. The university offers undergraduate and postgraduate degrees in various fields of study. The university is known for its high academic standards and excellent facilities. It has a reputation for producing graduates who are well-prepared for the workforce and are able to make a positive impact on society. The university is committed to providing a high-quality education that is accessible to all students, regardless of their background or financial situation. It is dedicated to promoting academic excellence, innovation, and social responsibility. The university is also known for its strong commitment to research and community engagement. It has a number of research centers and institutes that focus on a wide range of issues, including health, education, and the environment. The university is also involved in a number of community development projects that aim to improve the lives of people in Nigeria and beyond.

Our Mission
